Application Gate — The Private Briefing Model

Not a webinar. Not open registration. Families apply, IB reviews, 20 qualified seats are filled, then — and only then — the AO is contracted and paid. The room is full-pay qualified leads before a dollar goes to the AO.

Model Change

Old: Open registration webinar — anyone signs up, most are browsers.  New: Application-gated private briefing — 20 invited families, all qualified, all full-pay intent. AO cost is de-risked: only paid after seat 20 is confirmed. At 30% close on 20 families × $20K avg = $120K minimum per event.

Sales Ops & CRM — Proposed System

Right now the center is handpicking who to reach out to. No CRM, no tracking, no setter workflow. This replaces that — every lead touched, every touchpoint logged.

Current State

Lead submits form → goes into a void → someone manually decides who to call → no record of what happened → revenue is invisible. Leads are dying right now.
