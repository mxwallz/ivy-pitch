Sales Ops & CRM — Proposed System

Right now, the center is handpicking who to reach out to. There is no CRM. No way to know who confirmed a call, who booked, who showed. This replaces that with a system where the setter reaches out to every qualified lead and every touchpoint is tracked.

Current State

Lead submits form → goes into a void → someone manually decides who to call → no record of what happened → revenue is invisible. Leads are dying in the inbox right now.
