Application Gate — Private Briefing Model

Families apply to attend. IB reviews. 20 confirmed seats. AO gets paid after seat 20 — not before. Setter reaches out to every applicant regardless of acceptance status.

Model

Not a webinar. An invite-only private briefing. Families apply, IB reviews in 24hrs, accepted families get a personal invitation from Jake. Everyone who applies gets a setter touchpoint — accepted or not. AO is contracted only after 20 seats are locked.

Application Form Fields

  • Parent full name + email + phone
  • Student's current school
  • Student's current grade (8th–12th)
  • Country of origin
  • Country of residence
  • Target schools (up to 3)
  • Strongest current activity or project
  • Biggest concern about admissions
  • Worked with a consultant before? (Y/N)
  • How did you hear about Ivy Brothers?

Seat Routing — Soft, Not a Gate

  • Strong fit (11th–12th, Top-20 targets, detailed answers) → invite
  • Good fit but session full → priority waitlist for next run
  • Early timeline (8th–10th grade) → setter offers Hook Program, nurture until ready
  • International applicant → flag for AO premium angle + international email variant
  • Already worked with Crimson/Solomon → setter positions IB as the correction
  • Every applicant gets a setter call regardless of routing
